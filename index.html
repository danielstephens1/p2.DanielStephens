Pros vs Cons:

  • Pro: Bright lettering and numbering allows for easy readability

  • Con: Very little information avaliable and does not label anything to show important information
  • Pro: Controls are angled towards driver to allow easy access while driving

  • Con: Extreme clutter and being to the side of the steering wheel can be distracting and difficult to read speed while driving
  • Pro: Buttons on steering wheel allow for easy control, screen on dashboard is bright and gives a lot of information

  • Con: The monochrome color scheme makes the dashboard look cluttered, and can be confusing for drivers not use to the layout
  • Pro: Speedometer is large and centered

  • Con: All the controls and dials are in the center of the steering wheel with the controls and dials looking the same
  • Pro: Large speedometer and tachometer are easy to read

  • Con: The speedometer in the center of the dashboard forces the driver to turn their head while driving, which can be dangerous
Common Features:
  • Speedometer, Tachometer, and Odometer
  • Music controls
  • Gas Gauge
  • Warning lights
  • Windshield wipers
  • Turn sigals


Rare Uses:
  • Trip A/B selector
  • Cruise control
  • Temperature gauges


Mistakes:
  • A common mistake that can be made between car models is accidentally turning on the windshield wipers when trying to turn on headlights. Some designs have the headlight selector on the turn signal lever, where others have windshield wipers on the lever.

  • Another mistake is accidentally changing the song when trying to raise music volume. If the buttons on the steering wheel aren't large enough to be easily read, they can be difficult to differenciate between skip song and raise volume.

  • One of the most common mistakes is not pressing the turn signal lever hard enough to fully activate it. Some models have two options of turn signal, one being a three-second signal, and the other an indefinite signal.

Improved Dashboard Sketch:

Changes to Dashboard:

  • Music control buttons are clear and large to avoid confusion
  • A digital screen has been added to make reading speed easier
  • Turn signal is only one activation in each direction, avoids the signal turning off after 3 seconds
  • Headlights are large and clearly marked to avoid confusion between windshield wipers

With buttons being larger and clearly marked, most confusion is dissolved. All information needed is clearly presented and easily readable to the driver.
